Getting Here

While guests can fly into either O'Hare International Airport (ORD) or Chicago Midway International Airport (MDW), we recommend flying into O'Hare.

From the airport, there are two options:


1

The Van Galder bus picks up from both O'Hare and Midway, and will drop you off within walking distance from the Radisson Hotel, where we recommend guests to stay. The bus from O'Hare leaves approximately every hour while the bus from Midway leaves less frequently.


2

Rent a car. The drive to Rockford, IL takes approximately 1-1½ hours. Use Google Maps to help navigate the way!

Wedding Day! Saturday, June 13

Transportation to the Wedding

We have arranged for a 30-passenger bus to shuttle guests from the Radisson Hotel to Nicholas Conservatory, where both the wedding and reception will be held. This bus will leave the hotel for the Gardens at 3:30, 4:00, and 4:30, and will also be available for transport back to the hotels after the wedding. If guests are not staying at the Radisson Hotel, but a hotel nearby, feel free to walk over to the Radisson lobby for transportation!

Once you arrive at the venue, there is a trolley available to shuttle guests from the entrance to the wedding site. For those that would like to walk- please do! It is roughly a quarter mile.



Ceremony: 5:00pm

Please join us in the rose garden (weather pending!) as we say “I Do!” The ceremony will begin at 5:00pm and will be immediately followed by a cocktail hour. Parking is available.

For those driving, both the wedding ceremony and reception will be held at the Nicholas Conservatory, located at:

Nicholas Conservatory Gardens
1354 North 2nd St.
Rockford, IL 61107



Reception: 7:00-11:00pm

The cocktail hour will be immediately followed by a night of food, dancing, and fun! Please note that while after-dinner festivities will be indoors, we will likely eat on a tented patio, so dress appropriately. For those of you who have never been to the great Midwest, Rockford in June sees an average low of 58 and an average high of 81. Nights can be warm and humid!



After Party: 11:30pm

For guests that stay up past midnight, we will be hosting an after party at Alchemy, a bar and restaurant located right across the highway from our hotel. Please join us for tot-chos and drinks as we continue the celebration!
